The Agency
Behind the Voice

Born in London. Built for the modern man's world.

Marked Presence was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between premium fragrance and men's grooming brands and the authentic voices that move their audiences. We operate at the intersection of luxury lifestyle and digital influence.

Based in London — the heartbeat of men's style — we have built an unmatched network of creators who don't just promote products, they embody them. Our data-driven approach ensures every partnership delivers measurable, meaningful results.

Data-Driven Results

Authentic Relationships

We curate genuine creator partnerships — because audiences can spot inauthenticity, and luxury brands can't afford it.
